## FAQ Content

**Q: What time will flowers be delivered?**
A: Fresh flowers are delivered between 6 AM - 8 AM, perfect for morning puja.

**Q: When do you collect used flowers?**
A: We collect used flowers during the next day's delivery or at a scheduled time.

**Q: What happens to the used flowers?**
A: Used flowers are composted and converted into organic fertilizer, preventing river pollution.

**Q: Can I pause my subscription?**

**Q: What if I'm not home for delivery?**
A: You can specify a safe delivery location or reschedule through your account.

**Q: Are the flowers organic?**
A: Yes, we source fresh, naturally grown flowers from local farmers.
